🌱 About SocialSprouts

At SocialSprouts, we believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to connect, communicate, and grow confidently in their own way. Our platform is designed to support individuals on the autism spectrum by creating a safe, friendly, and engaging space for social interaction. Through visual communication tools, structured conversation guides, and sensory-friendly design, we help users build social skills at their own pace. SocialSprouts isn’t just a website—it’s a community that nurtures understanding, inclusion, and meaningful human connection.


✨ Key Features

AI Companion Chatbot

An intelligent chatbot designed to provide calm, structured conversation, answer social questions, and help practice communication skills in a safe, non-judgmental environment.

Interactive Quizzes

Fun, visual, and focused quizzes to help reinforce social cues, emotional recognition, and life skills. Track progress and build confidence in key developmental areas.

Task Scheduler

Create clear, predictable daily and weekly task schedules to reduce anxiety and manage transitions. Easily check off tasks and see what comes next.

Educational Video Streamer

Access a curated library of short, focused videos demonstrating social scenarios, self-care routines, and coping strategies in an easy-to-understand format.
